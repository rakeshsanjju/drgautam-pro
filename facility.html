Our Facilities

Avis Hospital is a multispeciality hospital located in Jubilee Hills, in the heart of Hyderabad. With state-of-the-art facilities in the OPD and consultation area, your privacy and confidentiality are fully assured. The operating theatre complex is equipped with the most up-to-date technology and instruments to handle complex surgeries efficiently and safely.

With a team of superspecialists available in all aspects of face, head, and neck care — including Maxillofacial Surgeon, Plastic Surgeon, Head and Neck Surgeon, ENT Surgeon, Oculoplastic Surgeon, Neurosurgeon, and Interventional Radiologist — it is possible to provide end-to-end care of the highest standards to patients with any kind of facial disorders, which we have successfully carried out over many years.
