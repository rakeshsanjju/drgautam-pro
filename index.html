Welcome to the world of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery! In simple terms, it is surgery for disorders and deformities of the mouth, jaws, and face.

Are you born with a facial defect like a cleft lip or palate? Does your kid have a congenital skull deformity? Have you lost your teeth and are unable to chew properly? Did you suffer an injury from an accident that caused your facial deformity?

Do you have a tumour or cyst in the jaw? Do you snore or have sleep apnoea? Do you want cosmetic surgery for your nose? If you do, I can help you restore normalcy, function, and confidence.

As an Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon with over 25 years of experience and clinical training from the UK and Ireland, you can be assured of safe, expert, and patient-centred care.
