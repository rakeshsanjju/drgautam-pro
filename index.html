Dr. Gautam Dendukuri Welcomes You

Dr Gautam graduated in dental surgery from Dr R Ahmed Dental college, the first dental college of the country. He then pursued higher surgical training in Oral and Maxillofacial surgery in the UK at world-renowned institutes like the Morriston Hospital (Swansea) and Queen Victoria Hospital (England).

He later worked as a registrar and locum Consultant at the Mid Western Regional Hospital, Limerick, Ireland. During this time, he acquired fellowships from the Royal College of Surgeons in England and Ireland.

Currently practicing in Hyderabad at Avis Hospital, Dr Gautam provides services in facial trauma repair, facial deformity correction, head and neck pathology, orthognathic surgery, obstructive sleep apnoea, and dental implant rehabilitation.
