Comprehensive Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery Services

FACIAL TRAUMA AND DEFORMITIES

If you have been an unfortunate victim of facial trauma as a result of an accident there is no need to feel helpless. At Avis hospital, the maxillofacial team led by Dr Gautam specialize in the correction of all these facial deformities.

If you have any of the following problems following an accident our team will be more than happy to offer appropriate advice:

  • Inability to bite or chew due to derangement of teeth alignment
  • Asymmetry of the face following trauma
  • Sunken eye appearance
  • Double vision
  • Flattening of the cheek bone area
  • Depression or deformity in the forehead area

Although patients following accidents should have immediate surgery, sometimes they may present late (secondary deformity) which also can be treated with proper planning and surgery. We also specialize in custom made, 3D printed and specially fabricated prosthesis for such secondary deformities.

ORTHOGNATHIC AND COSMETIC SURGERY

Do you feel your jaws are not in alignment? Are your jaws very prominent or very retrusive? Do you want your chin to be more prominent? All these problems can be addressed with a proper assessment, xrays and corrective surgery. Improve your confidence and self esteem with cosmetic orthognathic surgery.

At Avis hospital we also offer other cosmetic procedures like:

  • Rhinoplasty (nose correction)
  • Blepharoplasty
  • Face lift for facial rejuvenation

OBSTRUCTIVE SLEEP APNOEA

Do you have a problem with snoring? Do you have disturbed or inadequate sleep leading to tiredness during the day and unable to focus on work? You could be having obstructive sleep apnoea.

At Avis hospital we work in coordination with the ENT surgeons to provide a comprehensive treatment plan for sleep apnoea. This ranges from appliances that relieve apnoea to surgical procedures like:

  • Widening the upper jaw for better airway competence
  • Advancement of the jaws which is considered a gold standard in the treatment of obstructive sleep apnoea

HEAD AND NECK PATHOLOGY

Have you been diagnosed with a cyst or tumour in your jaws or any part of the mouth? This will need treatment from experts who are experienced in not only removal of the lesion but also reconstructive surgery to restore form and function.

At Avis hospital it is possible to get this kind of comprehensive treatment in safe hands with state of art equipment all under one roof!!

FULL MOUTH REHABILITATION

Do you have missing teeth or using removable dentures as a replacement for lost teeth? You can now say goodbye to those dentures and have a full set of fixed teeth with the help of Implants.

This can now be performed with precision using prefabricated digitally planned stents and minimally invasive surgery. Our prosthodontist will then fabricate the fixed prosthesis which a permanent solution to missing teeth. No more removing dentures and Happy chewing!!
