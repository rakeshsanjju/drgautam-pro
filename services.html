Comprehensive Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery Services

FACIAL TRAUMA AND DEFORMITIES

If you have been an unfortunate victim of facial trauma as a result of an accident there is no need to feel helpless. At Avis Hospital, the maxillofacial team led by Dr Gautam specialises in the correction of all facial deformities.

If you have any of the following problems following an accident, our team will be happy to help:

  • Inability to bite or chew due to derangement of teeth alignment
  • Asymmetry of the face following trauma
  • Sunken eye appearance
  • Double vision
  • Flattening of the cheekbone area
  • Depression or deformity in the forehead area

OBSTRUCTIVE SLEEP APNOEA

Snoring and interrupted sleep may indicate obstructive sleep apnoea. At Avis Hospital, we coordinate with ENT surgeons to deliver tailored treatments including appliances and advanced jaw-advancement procedures.

  • Upper jaw expansion for airway improvement
  • Jaw advancement surgery (gold standard treatment)
